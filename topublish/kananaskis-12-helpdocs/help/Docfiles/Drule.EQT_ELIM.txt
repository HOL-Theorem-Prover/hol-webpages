EQT_ELIM : (thm -> thm)

SYNOPSIS
Eliminates equality with {T}.

KEYWORDS
rule, truth.

DESCRIBE

    A |- tm = T
   -------------  EQT_ELIM
      A |- tm




FAILURE
Fails if the argument theorem is not of the form {A |- tm = T}.

SEEALSO
Drule.EQT_INTRO, Drule.EQF_ELIM, Drule.EQF_INTRO.
