pair                                                             (Lib)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
pair : 'a -> 'b -> 'a * 'b

SYNOPSIS
Makes two values into a pair.

DESCRIBE
{pair x y} returns {(x, y)}.

FAILURE
Never fails.
