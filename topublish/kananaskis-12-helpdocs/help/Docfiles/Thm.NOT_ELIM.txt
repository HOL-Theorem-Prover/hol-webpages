----------------------------------------------------------------------
NOT_ELIM                                                         (Thm)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
NOT_ELIM : thm -> thm

SYNOPSIS
Transforms {|- ~t} into {|- t ==> F}.

KEYWORDS
rule, implication, negation.

DESCRIBE
When applied to a theorem {A |- ~t}, the inference rule {NOT_ELIM} returns the
theorem {A |- t ==> F}.

      A |- ~t
   --------------  NOT_ELIM
    A |- t ==> F




FAILURE
Fails unless the theorem has a negated conclusion.

SEEALSO
Drule.IMP_ELIM, Thm.NOT_INTRO.
