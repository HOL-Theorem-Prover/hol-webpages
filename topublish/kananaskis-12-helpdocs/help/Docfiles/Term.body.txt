----------------------------------------------------------------------
body                                                            (Term)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
body : term -> term

SYNOPSIS
Returns the body of an abstraction.

DESCRIBE
If {M} is a lambda abstraction, i.e, has the form {\v. t}, then
{body M} returns {t}.

FAILURE
Fails unless {M} is an abstraction.

SEEALSO
Term.bvar, Term.dest_abs.
