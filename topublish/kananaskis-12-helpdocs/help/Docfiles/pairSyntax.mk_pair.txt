----------------------------------------------------------------------
mk_pair                                                   (pairSyntax)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
mk_pair : term * term -> term

SYNOPSIS
Constructs object-level pair from a pair of terms.

DESCRIBE
{mk_pair (t1,t2)} returns {(t1,t2)}.

FAILURE
Never fails.

SEEALSO
pairSyntax.dest_pair, pairSyntax.is_pair, pairSyntax.list_mk_pair.
