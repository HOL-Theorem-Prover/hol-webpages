----------------------------------------------------------------------
EQF_ELIM : (thm -> thm)

SYNOPSIS
Replaces equality with {F} by negation.

KEYWORDS
rule, negation, falsity.

DESCRIBE

    A |- tm = F
   -------------  EQF_ELIM
     A |- ~tm




FAILURE
Fails if the argument theorem is not of the form {A |- tm = F}.

SEEALSO
Drule.EQF_INTRO, Drule.EQT_ELIM, Drule.EQT_INTRO.
