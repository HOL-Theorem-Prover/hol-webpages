----------------------------------------------------------------------
swap                                                             (Lib)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
swap : 'a * 'b -> 'b * 'a

SYNOPSIS
Swaps the two components of a pair.

DESCRIBE
{swap (x,y)} returns {(y,x)}.

FAILURE
Never fails.

SEEALSO
Lib.fst, Lib.snd, Lib.pair, Lib.rpair.
