----------------------------------------------------------------------
CONJUNCT2                                                        (Thm)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
CONJUNCT2 : thm -> thm

SYNOPSIS
Extracts right conjunct of theorem.

KEYWORDS
rule, conjunction.

DESCRIBE

    A |- t1 /\ t2
   ---------------  CONJUNCT2
       A |- t2




FAILURE
Fails unless the input theorem is a conjunction.

COMMENTS
The theorem {AND2_THM} can be instantiated to similar effect.

SEEALSO
Drule.BODY_CONJUNCTS, Thm.CONJUNCT1, Drule.CONJ_PAIR, Thm.CONJ,
Drule.LIST_CONJ, Drule.CONJ_LIST, Drule.CONJUNCTS.
