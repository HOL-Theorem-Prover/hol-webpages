rpair                                                            (Lib)

rpair : 'a -> 'b -> 'b * 'a

SYNOPSIS
Makes two values into a pair, in reverse order.

DESCRIBE
{rpair x y} returns {(y,x)}.

FAILURE
Never fails.

SEEALSO
Lib.pair, Lib.swap, Lib.fst, Lib.snd, Lib.curry, Lib.uncurry.
