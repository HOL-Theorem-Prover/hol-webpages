num_CONV                                                      (numLib)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
num_CONV : conv

SYNOPSIS
Equates a non-zero numeral with the form {SUC x} for some {x}.

EXAMPLE

- num_CONV ``1203``;
> val it = |- 1203 = SUC 1202 : thm


FAILURE
Fails if the argument term is not a numeral of type {``:num``}, or if
the argument is {``0``}.

SEEALSO
numLib.SUC_TO_NUMERAL_DEFN_CONV.
