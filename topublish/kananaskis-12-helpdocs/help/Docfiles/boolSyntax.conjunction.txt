conjunction                                               (boolSyntax)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
conjunction : term

SYNOPSIS
Constant denoting logical conjunction.

LIBRARY
boolSyntax

DESCRIBE
The ML variable {boolSyntax.conjunction} is bound to the term {bool$/\}.
