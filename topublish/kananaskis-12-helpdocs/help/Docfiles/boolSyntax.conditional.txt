conditional                                               (boolSyntax)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
conditional : term

SYNOPSIS
Constant denoting conditional expressions.

LIBRARY
boolSyntax

DESCRIBE
The ML variable {boolSyntax.conditional} is bound to the term {bool$COND}.
