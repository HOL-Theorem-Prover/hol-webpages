implication                                               (boolSyntax)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
implication : term

SYNOPSIS
Constant denoting logical implication.

LIBRARY
boolSyntax

DESCRIBE
The ML variable {boolSyntax.implication} is bound to the term {min$==>}.
