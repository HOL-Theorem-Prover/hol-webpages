REFL                                                             (Thm)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
REFL : conv

SYNOPSIS
Returns theorem expressing reflexivity of equality.

KEYWORDS
rule, reflexive, equality.

DESCRIBE
{REFL} maps any term {t} to the corresponding theorem {|- t = t}.

FAILURE
Never fails.

SEEALSO
Conv.ALL_CONV, Tactic.REFL_TAC.
