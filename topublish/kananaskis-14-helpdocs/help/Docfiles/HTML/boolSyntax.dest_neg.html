dest_neg : term -> term
STRUCTURE
boolSyntax
SYNOPSIS
Breaks apart a negation, returning its body.
DESCRIPTION
dest_neg is a term destructor for negations: if M has the form ~t, then dest_neg M returns t.
FAILURE
Fails with dest_neg if term is not a negation.
SEEALSO
mk_neg, is_neg
