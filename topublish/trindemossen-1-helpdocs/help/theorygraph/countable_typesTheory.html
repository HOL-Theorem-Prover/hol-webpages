Theory "countable_types"

Parents     indexedLists   patternMatches

Theorems

countable_split 
⊢ ∀X f m.
    INJ f X 𝕌(:num list # α list) ∧
    (∀x y. x ∈ X ∧ MEM y (SND (f x)) ⇒ y ∈ X ∧ m y < m x) ⇒
    countable X
list_countable 
⊢ countable Lset ⇔ countable (BIGUNION (IMAGE LIST_TO_SET Lset))
unit_countable 
⊢ countable 𝕌(:unit)
